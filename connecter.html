Hi, my name is Henry Mann and back in 2024 I created my North Jersey Fire Buffs Youtube account. Later in 2025, I created my TikTok account which has been growing rapidly averaging 3+ followers per day. In February of 2026, I decided to create an Instagram account to boost my following. Creating North Jersey Fire Buffs has allowed me to express my love for firefighters and photography and share it with the public. On my social accounts, I post videos of emergency vehicles responding, operating on scenes, returning from calls and I photograph & film fire scenes.
